Karolina Dziadura

Postdoctoral researcher at the Center for Astrophysics | Harvard & Smithsonian.
I specialize in asteroid orbit determination, celestial mechanics, and astrometry. I am also experienced in telescope observations and data analysis using Gaia space telescope.

Asteroid Research

My research focuses on high-precision orbit determination and the Yarkovsky effect using data from missions like Gaia. I aim to understand non-gravitational forces influencing small Solar System bodies.

Media & Outreach

I am part of the Good Night Collective, a science-art initiative blending creative storytelling with space science. I also co-host a science podcast aired on Radio Afera every Saturday at 13:00.
