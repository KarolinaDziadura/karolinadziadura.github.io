Karolina Dziadura, PhD

Postdoctoral Researcher at the Minor Planet Center,
Center for Astrophysics | Harvard & Smithsonian

I am an astrophysicist working on asteroid orbits, celestial mechanics, and astrometric data analysis. My work includes using Gaia data and ground-based observations to investigate the Yarkovsky effect, orbit refinement, and photocenter offsets. Currently, I am focusing on a statistical analysis of observatory performance in order to develop a complex weighting scheme for automated and improved orbit determination.

Research

My main research interests involve:

  • Statystical analysis or astrometry residuals
  • Yarkovsky effect detection and modeling
  • Photocenter-barycenter offset analysis
  • Asteroid orbit precision and long-arc astrometry
